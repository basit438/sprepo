Bat: Used by the batsman to hit the ball.
Ball: Used by the bowler to bowl and by the batsman to hit.
Stumps: Consist of three vertical posts topped by bails. Placed at each end of the pitch, they are used to determine if a batsman is out.
Pads: Worn by the batsman to protect the legs from being hit by the ball.
Gloves: Worn by the wicketkeeper and batsmen for protection and better grip.
Helmet: Worn by batsmen and fielders for head protection.
Thigh guards: Worn by batsmen for additional leg protection.
Chest guard: Worn by batsmen for protection against fast bowling.
Arm guard: Worn by batsmen for protection against fast bowling.
Abdominal guard: Worn by batsmen for protection against fast bowling.
